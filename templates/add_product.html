How to manage product?

- Add a product with its basic information like Product title, description, specifications , product SKU, tax, and product variants.

- Once you create a Product it will viewable from all Branches. But if there is no stock in a specific Branch, the Branch will not be able to sell the product.

- Manage products' other information like price, barcode, discount, tax, etc, from the Stock module. Stock is manage by a particular branch. Manage Stock by adding specific Lot to a specific Branch.

- After a Stock is received (status: Received), Those products will automatically add to your Stock
